About Us

Hey there. Welcome to Lovely Plant Shop. A little bit about us...

We created Lovely Plant Shop in 2019 because, quite simply, we love plants. When launching our shop, we wanted to create a space that felt warm and welcoming to all, from plant admirers to expert growers. One of our favorite parts of operating Grow Plant Shop is sourcing the most beautiful plants that we can get our hands on, and of course sharing them with all of you.

Lovely Plant Shop started in Indianapolis, Indiana and was originally operated out of a small greenhouse in our backyard. In January 2021, we expanded to our brick and mortar shop located at 1111 First Ave. In our shop, you'll find a carefully curated selection of both indoor and garden plants, planters, homemade ceramics, garden tools, handmade baskets, and home goods.
